Our Family Story

Once Upon a Time...

It all began in the spring of 2008 when Sarah and Michael met at a friend's wedding. What started as a chance encounter quickly blossomed into a beautiful love story.

We married in 2010 and welcomed our first child, Emma, in 2012. Our family grew with the arrival of Noah in 2014 and Olivia in 2017.

Today, we're creating memories together, traveling, exploring nature, and learning new things every day. Our family motto is "Love grows here," and we live by those words every single day.
